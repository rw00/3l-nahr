About us FAQs
Sereno means serenity in Spanish. This is, indeed, the experience we provide to you at our park.
Good times are guaranteed at Sereno.
We welcome weddings and events at our open venue during the warm season. For more information about pricing, please contact us.
What is the venue capacity?

We can seat up to 350 people. A large area remains available as a dance floor. There's also a stage for event performances.

What services do you provide?

We can plan your wedding or event in collaboration with our DJ, light and catering partners.
We also sell cold and hot beverages.
